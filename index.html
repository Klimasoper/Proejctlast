Recommendations

1.choice of colors. Before you start decorating the Christmas tree, choose the color scheme that will prevail. It can be a traditional red and green, stylish black and white, delicate pink and blue, or any other combination that you like.

2.main toy. Choose the main toy for the Christmas tree, which will become the center of attention. It can be a large sparkling star or an angel on top of the Christmas tree, which will give it a complete look.

3.Use a variety of decorations. Do not limit yourself to toys, add garlands, confetti, beads, balls, bows, snowflakes and other decorative elements to the decoration of the Christmas tree

Play with textures. Add to the textures in the decor, for example, the use of artificial snow, glass balls, velvet ribbons, feathers or glitter.

5Distribute the decorations evenly. To make the tree look symmetrical and harmonious, distribute the decorations evenly throughout the tree. Do not load it too much so that it remains airy and light.
